# Weqaya - Smart Medical Records AI

**Project Overview**  
Weqaya is a secure medical records application designed to empower patients by giving them full control over their health history. Inspired by the saying "Prevention is better than cure," it centralizes medical information, making healthcare safer and more efficient.

**Key Features**
- **Centralized Health Records:** Store and access medical history anytime, anywhere.  
- **AI Chatbot:** Analyze symptoms for faster and smarter health insights.  
- **OCR Technology:** Digitize handwritten prescriptions easily.

**Why It Matters**  
Incomplete or inaccessible patient data can lead to misdiagnoses and dangerous drug interactions. Weqaya reduces these risks, streamlines care, and saves time for both patients and medical staff.

**Folder Overview**
- `MedicalPrescriptionReader`: Extract and structure data from handwritten medical prescriptions.  
- `SmartMedicalChatbot`: Provide accurate and efficient medical information using AI.
